Job Board / PPF

Paint Protection Film

Basic / Standard / Premium / Ultimate

Once booked, we’ll call you with a date and time for an initial appointment to pick your color and confirm other details.

Paint protection film (PPF) is a clear film that is applied to your vehicles painted surfaces that protects against stone chips, scratches and other environmental factors. We can do PPF on anything from partial pieces of a vehicle to an entire vehicle top to bottom. Once the PPF is installed, the result will be a factory-like finish. Since we tuck all of the edges where possible, it is hard to tell that it is even on your vehicle. The film has self-healing properties which allow small scratches, swirls and defects to be removed with heat. Road grime, bird droppings, bugs, and air pollution will never leave the film stained or yellow, unlike some lower quality alternatives. With its thermoplastic chemistry, the semi-crystalline surface of our PPF remains flexible, and conforms to the body of your vehicle for the perfect fit.
You are guaranteed excellent service for up to 10 years after installation without cracking or peeling warranted by the XPEL. Further, we extend this warranty for 10 years to include workmanship without waiting for a claim decision. We ensure you have the best possible installation by servicing all vehicles on a lift at eye level. Moreover, our installation services include different stages of vehicle preparation including washing, clay and buff whenever necessary. Upon your request, we can repaint your vehicle's surface before installation.
It is also an investment in preserving the value of your vehicle. If the Paint Protection Film remains unbroken, the paint beneath will look good as new, and saving it from any small damages that could cost you thousands.
